Why Visit Ecuador and Cuba?

Two destinations full of culture, nature, and unforgettable experiences.

Ecuador

  • Diverse nature: Andes mountains, Amazon rainforest, and the Galápagos Islands.
  • Rich culture: Indigenous traditions, colorful markets, and colonial cities like Quito.
  • Great food: Fresh ceviche, locro de papa, and tropical fruits.

Cuba

  • Unique history: Classic cars, historic Havana, and vibrant architecture.
  • Music and dance: Salsa rhythms and live music in every corner.
  • Beautiful beaches: Crystal-clear waters and white sand in places like Varadero.
