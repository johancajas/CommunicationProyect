Visit Latin America — Cuba & Ecuador

Two compact but endlessly rich destinations: vibrant music, colonial heritage, and Caribbean beaches in Cuba — and staggering biodiversity, Andean peaks, and the Galápagos in Ecuador. Explore both for a culturally and naturally diverse trip.

Scroll down to explore each destination and discover what makes them so unforgettable.

Why travel to Cuba & Ecuador?

Both countries offer a concentrated set of experiences: music, history, and classic Caribbean shores in Cuba; mountain treks, Amazon adventures, and the unique Galápagos in Ecuador. This site collects practical planning info, highlights, and quick-start resources so you can move from dreaming to planning.

Cuba — music, history & beaches

  • Unique history: Classic cars, historic Havana, and vibrant architecture.
  • Music & dance: Salsa rhythms and live music in every corner.
  • Beaches & relaxation: Crystal-clear waters and white sand in places like Varadero.

Cuba is a country of vivid history, captivating landscapes, and deeply rooted cultural traditions. From Havana’s colonial streets to the beaches of Varadero, the island offers a uniquely musical and laid-back Caribbean experience.

Ecuador — Andes, Amazon & Galápagos

  • Diverse nature: Andes mountains, Amazon rainforest, and the Galápagos Islands.
  • Rich culture: Indigenous traditions, colorful markets, and colonial cities like Quito.
  • Great food: Fresh ceviche, locro de papa, and tropical fruits.

Ecuador is one of the most biodiverse countries on Earth, where towering Andean peaks meet lush Amazon rainforest and the extraordinary wildlife of the Galápagos. Its compact size makes it easy to experience a lot in a short trip.
